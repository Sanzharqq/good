Suit

Suit is a type of clothing that consists of a jacket and trousers, typically worn in formal or business settings. It is characterized by its tailored fit, often with a matching waistcoat or vest. Suits are available in various styles, colors, and fabrics, ranging from classic black and white to more colorful options. They are designed to be worn together as a coordinated outfit, often with matching accessories such as ties, pocket squares, and shoes.
